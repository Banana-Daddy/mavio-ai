Inside Mavio

Every part chosen on purpose.

Two brushless motors. Four polyurethane wheels (the second redesign — the first wore out and we said so publicly). One green PCB. One CMOS camera module. Two anodized aluminum brackets. Hardware lead by Ray Shen, twenty years of embedded systems.

Operating loop

Capture · Analyze · Adapt · Improve.

A single closed loop, every shot. A coach's job, automated honestly — no ChatGPT hand-waving.

01
Capture

Computer vision tracks movement, posture, footwork, and ball trajectory in real time.

02
Analyze

AI identifies weaknesses, surfaces strengths, and finds optimal training patterns for your game.

03
Adapt

The machine adjusts speed, spin, and placement in real time. Drills get harder as you get better.

04
Improve

Personalized feedback, targeted sessions, and a development roadmap that doesn't reset every week.
