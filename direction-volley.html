From the founder

A solo project. A 3D printer. A tennis problem.

"Coaching was crazy expensive. Hitting partners flaked. And ball machines? The ones I tried felt like relics from the 90s."

SL
Sophie Luo · Founder
UPenn CS · ex-AR-glasses unicorn

Mavio started in March 2025. Sophie hacked together early prototypes, wrote the first computer-vision code, and 3D-printed brackets on her living room floor. She'd just finished leading product at an AI + AR-glasses startup that became a unicorn.

Six months later: a hardware lead with twenty years of embedded systems (Ray Shen), three field tests, a redesigned launch wheel, and the first integration between machine and AI app — under way as you read this.
