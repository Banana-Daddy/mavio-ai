Logo Concepts — 3 directions

v1.0 · 04.30.26

Three logo systems for Mavio. Each carries a wordmark, an icon mark, a monogram for app/favicon use, and a single-letter "stamp" for partnerships and packaging. Designed in vector — every variation is production-ready.

Concept 01

Trace

A continuous single-stroke wordmark — the dot of the i is the ball, traced by a flight arc that resolves the entire mark. Quietly editorial. Reads as craft, not robot.

Geometry · Variable weight · Scales 16–1200px

Spec

Engineered grotesque — a precise mechanical wordmark with a hatched dot and a stamp-sized "M·V" technical badge. Reads like an engineering drawing in a Hasselblad manual.

Volley
